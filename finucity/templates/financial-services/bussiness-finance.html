Capital That Works for You

In the modern Indian economy, access to the right kind of capital at the right time is as decisive as the business idea itself. We move beyond traditional banking relationships to offer a comprehensive spectrum of financial instruments — from optimizing working capital cycles and securing collateral-free growth funds under CGTMSE, to structuring complex project finance and facilitating startup seed funding through angel networks.

Our approach is data-driven and compliance-linked. Every recommendation is backed by financial modeling, credit analysis, and regulatory compliance checks. We don't just help you get loans — we help you build a creditworthy balance sheet that progressively reduces your cost of borrowing.

MSME Ecosystem

Udyam Registration

Gateway to all government benefits — priority sector lending, subsidies, and reduced patent/trademark fees. Free online registration with Aadhaar.

Priority Sector Lending

Banks mandated to lend 40% of net credit to priority sectors. MSMEs get loans at lower-than-market rates with reduced processing fees.

PMEGP & CLCSS Subsidies

Prime Minister's Employment Generation Programme offers 15-35% capital subsidy. CLCSS provides 15% subsidy on institutional finance for technology upgradation.

ZED Certification

Zero Defect Zero Effect certification — subsidized up to 80% under MSME scheme. Enhances export competitiveness and brand value on global platforms.

🏦

Debt Syndication

Working Capital Finance

Cash Credit (CC) & Overdraft (OD) limits structured against inventory and receivables. Typical interest: MCLR + 1-3% for rated companies.

Term Loans

Long-term financing for capex, machinery, and infrastructure. Tenure up to 7-10 years with moratorium periods. Floating and fixed rate options.

Project Finance

Large-ticket funding for infrastructure and greenfield projects. Structured as limited-recourse financing with SPV structures. Debt-equity ratios optimized per industry norms.

Loan Against Property (LAP)

High-value secured funding at competitive rates (8.5-11%). LTV ratios up to 60-70% of market value. Used for business expansion without diluting equity.

🚀

Startup & Growth Capital

Seed Funding Access

Connections to angel investor networks (Indian Angel Network, Mumbai Angels, LetsVenture). Average seed round: ₹50L–₹2Cr for validated ideas.

Startup India Benefits

DPIIT recognition unlocks 3-year tax holiday under Sec 80-IAC, self-certification under 6 labour laws, fast-tracked patent filings at 80% rebate.

Venture Debt

Non-dilutive growth capital for scaling operations without giving up equity. Typical structure: 12-36 month tenure with warrants. Ideal for post-Series A companies.

Revenue-Based Financing

Monthly repayment as a percentage of revenue (3-8%). No fixed EMI — payments flex with business performance. Perfect for SaaS and subscription businesses.

🌎

Trade Finance

Letter of Credit (LC)

Secure domestic and international trade. Sight LC, Usance LC, and Standby LC structures. Covers buyer default risk for sellers and supplier delivery risk for buyers.

Bank Guarantees

Performance guarantees, financial guarantees, and advance payment guarantees. Essential for government tenders and infrastructure contracts.

Export Credit

Pre-shipment (Packing Credit) and Post-shipment finance in foreign currency. Interest subvention of 2-3% available for certain export sectors under RBI scheme.

Forex Risk Management

Natural hedging, forward contracts, and options strategies to protect margins from currency volatility. Critical for businesses with USD/EUR receivables or payables.
