Philosophy

Insurance is Not Investment

The single biggest financial mistake Indians make is mixing insurance with investment. ULIPs, endowment plans, and money-back policies provide neither adequate insurance nor competitive returns. A ₹25L endowment plan costs ₹50,000/year — but a ₹1Cr pure term plan costs just ₹12,000/year. The ₹38,000 difference, if invested in a simple index fund at 12% CAGR, creates ₹1.1 Crore wealth over 25 years.

Our philosophy is crystal clear: Buy Term, Invest the Difference (BTID). Insurance should only serve one purpose — risk transfer. We use the Human Life Value (HLV) method to calculate the exact coverage needed based on your income, liabilities, future obligations, and existing assets. Every policy recommendation is data-backed, not commission-driven.

Term Life Insurance

Coverage Calculation

HLV Method: (Annual Income × Working Years Remaining) - (Existing Assets + EPF/PPF Corpus). Add all liabilities (Home Loan, Education Loans). Typical recommendation: 15-20x annual income for ages 25-35.

Key Features to Prioritize

Claim Settlement Ratio > 97%, lump sum + monthly income payout options, whole life coverage option (till 99 years), terminal illness benefit, and waiver of premium rider for disability.

Cost Optimization

Buy early (25-30 age): ₹1Cr cover at ₹8,000-12,000/year. Add 3-5% annual income step-up rider to match salary growth. Stagger policies: ₹50L till 45 + ₹50L till 60 instead of single ₹1Cr till 60.

Top Considerations

Compare LIC, HDFC Life, ICICI Pru, Max Life, Tata AIA based on CSR, solvency ratio, and premium. Non-smoker, non-tobacco status saves 30-40% on premiums. Medical tests are mandatory above ₹50L cover.

Health Insurance

Base + Super Top-Up Strategy

₹5-10L base health plan + ₹25-50L Super Top-Up with deductible equal to base plan. Total coverage: ₹30-60L at fraction of cost. Super Top-Up premium for ₹50L: just ₹3,000-5,000/year.

Essential Features Checklist

Room rent: No capping/No sub-limits. Restoration benefit (recharges sum insured after full utilization). Day care procedures coverage (490+ listed). No co-payment across all ages. AYUSH treatment coverage.

Family Floater vs Individual

Family floater (covers entire family under one sum insured) works till age 45. After 45, separate individual policies for parents recommended — parental claims should not reduce family's coverage.

Tax Benefits

Section 80D: ₹25,000 deduction for self/family premium. Additional ₹25,000 for parents' premium (₹50,000 if parents are senior citizens). Preventive health check-up: ₹5,000 within limits.

Critical Illness Cover

Why Separate Critical Illness

Health insurance covers hospitalization, but cancer treatment costs ₹20-40L over 2-3 years including travel, diet, caregiving, income loss. Critical Illness provides lump sum payout on diagnosis — covers non-medical costs health insurance cannot.

Coverage Amount

Recommend 2-3x annual income as Critical Illness cover. If earning ₹20L/year, take ₹50L CI cover. Lump sum payout on diagnosis of covered conditions (heart attack, stroke, cancer, kidney failure, major organ transplant).

Must-Cover Conditions

Ensure policy covers: Cancer (all stages), Heart Attack, Stroke, Kidney Failure, Major Organ Transplant, Coronary Bypass, Paralysis, Multiple Sclerosis. Choose plans covering 30+ conditions minimum.

Asset Protection

Home Insurance

Covers structure + contents against fire, flood, earthquake, theft, and burglary. Premium: ₹2,000-5,000/year for ₹50L-1Cr coverage. Mandatory for home loan (structure insurance).

Motor Insurance

Third-party liability is mandatory by law. Own Damage (OD) covers your vehicle. Go for comprehensive cover with add-ons: Zero Depreciation, Engine Protect, Return to Invoice. NCB protection to preserve no-claim bonus discount (up to 50% premium).

Personal Accident Cover

Covers accidental death, permanent total/partial disability, temporary total disability. ₹50L-1Cr cover at ₹3,000-5,000/year. Complements term life insurance for accident-specific scenarios.
