üí° Insurance Planning Tips

1. Buy Term, Invest the Rest

Choose pure term insurance for life coverage and invest the savings in mutual funds or other growth instruments separately.

2. Adequate Health Cover

Secure at least ‚Çπ5-10 lakhs health insurance per family member, adjusting for inflation and rising healthcare costs.

3. Start Early

Purchase insurance policies early in life to lock in lower premiums and ensure maximum coverage period.

4. Disclose Everything

Always provide complete and accurate medical history to avoid claim rejections during emergencies.

5. Review Regularly

Review your insurance portfolio annually or after major life events like marriage, childbirth, or job changes.

6. Consider Inflation

Factor in medical inflation when selecting health insurance coverage and opt for policies with inflation protection.
