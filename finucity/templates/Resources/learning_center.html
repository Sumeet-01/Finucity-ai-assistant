Essential Financial Concepts

💰 Compounding

₹10,000/month at 12% for 25 years = ₹1.89 Crore. That's the magic — your money earns money on its earnings.

📈 Inflation

At 6% inflation, ₹1 Crore today is worth ₹31L in 20 years. Your investments must beat inflation to create real wealth.

🌱 Rule of 72

Divide 72 by your return rate to find doubling time. At 12% return, money doubles every 6 years (72 ÷ 12 = 6).

🎯 Asset Allocation

Don't put all eggs in one basket. A mix of Equity (high growth) + Debt (stability) + Gold (hedge) reduces risk without sacrificing returns.

🔒 Emergency Fund

Keep 6 months of expenses in liquid funds/savings. This prevents you from breaking investments during unexpected financial shocks.

📊 Expense Ratio

A 1% higher expense ratio costs ₹25-30L over 25 years on a ₹1Cr portfolio. Always choose low-cost index and direct plans.
