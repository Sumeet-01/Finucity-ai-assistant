How accurate is Finucity AI's financial advice?

Finucity AI maintains a 98.5% accuracy rate for financial information. Our AI is continuously trained on the latest tax laws, financial regulations, and best practices in India. However, for legally binding decisions, we always recommend consulting with a human professional.

Is my financial information secure with Finucity?

Absolutely. We employ bank-level encryption for all conversations and never store sensitive financial details. Your privacy is our priority, and we adhere to the strictest data protection standards to ensure your information remains confidential.

What types of financial questions can I ask?

You can ask about virtually any financial topic relevant to India, including income tax planning, GST compliance, investment strategies, retirement planning, business structuring, and more. Our AI is designed to handle both simple queries and complex financial scenarios.

How is Finucity different from just searching online?

Unlike general web searches that return multiple sources with potentially conflicting information, Finucity AI provides a single, clear answer tailored to your specific situation. Our AI synthesizes information from authoritative sources and applies it to your unique context, saving you hours of research and interpretation.

Can Finucity AI help with my business finances?

Yes! Finucity AI is equipped to handle a wide range of business financial queries, from GST compliance and input tax credit optimization to business structure recommendations and tax-efficient profit distribution strategies. Whether you're a freelancer, small business owner, or startup founder, our AI can provide valuable guidance.

Is Finucity suitable for complex financial planning?

While Finucity AI can handle many complex financial questions, we always recommend consulting with professional financial advisors for major financial decisions. Our AI serves as an excellent starting point and educational resource, but human expertise is still valuable for complex personalized planning.

How often is the financial information updated?

Our AI is regularly updated with the latest financial regulations, tax codes, and market information. We make sure to incorporate new budget announcements, tax amendments, and regulatory changes as soon as they are officially released.

Yes, registered users can access their complete conversation history. This feature allows you to refer back to previous advice and continue conversations where you left off, making it easier to build on earlier discussions about your financial situation.
