How we protect your financial data and conversations

Our Security Commitment

At Finucity, we prioritize the security and privacy of your financial information above everything else. Our platform employs industry-leading security measures to ensure your data remains protected at all times.

End-to-End Encryption

All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.3 protocol, ensuring that your financial information cannot be intercepted or accessed by unauthorized parties.

Data Privacy

We minimize data collection to only what's necessary for providing our services. Your conversation history is encrypted and securely stored, and you maintain full control over your data.

Secure Authentication

Our multi-layered authentication system protects your account from unauthorized access. We enforce strong password policies and support secure session management.

Regular Security Audits

Our code and infrastructure undergo regular security audits and vulnerability assessments to identify and address potential security issues before they can be exploited.

Our Data Practices

Data Storage

We store minimal personal data, only what's necessary to provide our financial advisory services. All stored data is encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption.

Data Retention

Your conversation history is retained for as long as your account is active, allowing you to refer back to previous advice. You can request data deletion at any time.

Data Sharing

We never sell your data or share it with third parties without your explicit consent. Your financial information remains strictly confidential.

Access Controls

Only authorized personnel with specific roles have access to our systems, following the principle of least privilege to ensure data security.

Steps You Can Take

While we implement robust security measures, you can enhance the security of your account by following these recommendations:

1

Use Strong Passwords

Create a unique, complex password for your Finucity account. Consider using a password manager to generate and store secure passwords.

2

Keep Devices Secure

Ensure your devices have the latest security updates and use reputable antivirus software. Avoid using public or shared computers for accessing sensitive financial information.

3

Be Vigilant About Phishing

Be cautious of emails or messages asking for your Finucity credentials. We will never ask for your password via email or messages.

4

Sign Out When Finished

Always log out of your Finucity account when you're done, especially when using shared devices or public computers.

Security Concerns?

If you believe you've found a security vulnerability or have concerns about the security of your account, please contact us immediately at:

security@finucity.ai | GitHub | LinkedIn
