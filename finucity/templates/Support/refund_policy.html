03Non-Refundable Cases

  • Services that have been fully delivered and consumed as described
  • CA consultations where the CA has provided the agreed-upon service
  • Refund requests made after the applicable refund window
  • Accounts terminated for violation of Terms of Service
  • Change of mind after using/accessing the paid service content
  • Government filing fees paid on behalf of the user (ITR, GST, etc.)

04Cancellation Policy

  • Subscription Services: Cancel anytime from account settings. Access continues until the end of the billing period. No partial-month refunds after the 14-day window.
  • CA Engagements: Cancel before the CA begins work for a full refund. Partial refund if work is partially completed, proportional to work done.
  • Scheduled Consultations: Cancel 24 hours before the scheduled time for a full refund. No-shows are not refundable.

05Dispute Resolution

If you're not satisfied with our refund decision, you may:

  • Request an escalation to our senior management team
  • Provide additional documentation supporting your refund request
  • Contact us via our dispute resolution email for further review

We are committed to fair resolution and will consider all cases individually.
