Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent refund practices

Effective Date: January 1, {{ current_year }} • Last Updated: {{ current_date }}

1. Overview

At Finucity, we strive to deliver the highest quality AI-powered financial advisory services. If you are not satisfied with a paid service, we offer refunds under the conditions outlined below.

2. Free Services

Our core AI chat advisory service is free to use. Since no payment is collected for these services, no refund applies. This includes:

  • AI-powered tax and financial Q&A
  • Tax calculators and financial tools
  • General financial guidance
  • Learning resources and articles

3. Paid CA Services

For consultation bookings with verified Chartered Accountants:

  • Before CA assignment: Full refund available within 48 hours of booking
  • After CA assignment, before consultation: 80% refund if cancelled 24 hours prior to scheduled time
  • After consultation begins: Refunds evaluated on a case-by-case basis
  • Service not delivered: Full refund if the assigned CA fails to deliver within the agreed timeframe

Note: All refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the original transaction date.

4. How to Request a Refund

To initiate a refund, follow these steps:

  • Log in to your Finucity account
  • Navigate to your booking/consultation history
  • Select the transaction and click "Request Refund"
  • Provide a brief reason for the refund request

Alternatively, email us at support@finucity.com with your booking details.

Once approved, refunds are processed as follows:

  • You will receive email confirmation once initiated

6. Non-Refundable Items

  • Government fees paid on your behalf (GST registration, ITR filing fees, etc.)
  • Third-party charges
  • Services already completed and delivered
  • Subscriptions after the current billing period

7. Disputes

If you believe a refund decision is unfair, you can raise a dispute through our support channel. We will review your case within 5 business days and provide a resolution.
