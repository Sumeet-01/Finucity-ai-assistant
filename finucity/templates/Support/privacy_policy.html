Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fundamental to us. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last Updated: January 2025 | Effective: January 2025

Contents

01Introduction

Finucity ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, use our AI financial advisor, calculators, or any of our services (collectively, the "Services").

By accessing or using our Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our Services.

02Information We Collect

Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and password when you create an account
  • Financial Inputs: Income figures, deduction amounts, and other financial data you enter into our calculators and AI advisor
  • Chat Conversations: Messages exchanged with our AI financial advisor
  • Contact Information: Name, email, and message content when you use our contact form
  • CA Registration: Professional credentials, ICAI membership details, and identity documents for Chartered Accountants

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent, click patterns (anonymized)
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution, language preferences
  • Log Data: IP address (anonymized), access times, referring URLs
  • Cookies: Session cookies for authentication and preference cookies for user experience (see Section 8)
Important: Calculator inputs are processed client-side (in your browser) and are NOT stored on our servers unless you explicitly save them to your account. Your tax calculations remain private.

03How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our Services
  • To personalize your experience and provide relevant financial guidance
  • To process your AI advisor queries and deliver accurate responses
  • To connect you with verified Chartered Accountants when requested
  • To send important service updates, security alerts, and account notifications
  • To analyze usage patterns (anonymized) and improve our platform
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, and ensure platform security
  • To comply with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
We do NOT use your data for: Targeted advertising, selling to third parties, training AI models on your private conversations, or any purpose beyond providing and improving our Services.

04Information Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

  • AI Service Providers: Your chat queries are processed through secure API channels to AI providers (OpenAI, Google, Anthropic) under strict data processing agreements. Only the minimum necessary context is shared.
  • CA Consultations: When you initiate a consultation, relevant information is shared with the selected CA only with your explicit consent.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation.
  • Service Providers: Trusted infrastructure providers (Supabase, hosting) who process data on our behalf under confidentiality agreements.

05Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your data:

  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • TLS 1.3 encryption for data in transit
  • PostgreSQL Row Level Security (RLS) policies
  • Bcrypt password hashing with high salt rounds
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Automatic session logout and secure cookie handling

While we strive to protect your information using commercially acceptable means, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but continuously improve our defenses.

06Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request.
  • Chat History: Retained while your account is active. You can delete individual conversations at any time.
  • Calculator Inputs: Not stored on servers (processed client-side) unless explicitly saved.
  • Contact Form Submissions: Retained for 1 year, then securely deleted.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymized data retained for up to 2 years for service improvement.

07Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate personal information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a structured, commonly used format
  • Objection: Object to certain types of data processing
  • Withdrawal of Consent: Withdraw consent for data processing at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@finucity.com. We will respond within 30 days.

08Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies to enhance your experience:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for authentication, session management, and security. Cannot be disabled.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings like theme (dark/light mode), language, and display preferences.
  • Analytics Cookies: Anonymized usage data to understand how our platform is used and improve it. Can be disabled.

We do NOT use advertising or third-party tracking cookies. You can manage cookies through your browser settings.

09Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected data from a child under 18, we will take immediate steps to delete such information.

10Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will notify you by posting a prominent notice on our platform and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of our Services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

Email: privacy@finucity.com

General Support: support@finucity.com

Response Time: Within 30 days for privacy-related requests
